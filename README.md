Swadhikar (स्वाधिकार)

Your Rights. Your Voice. Your Country.

Swadhikar is a conversational web platform designed to help citizens understand their legal rights, government schemes, and farmer benefits through a simple WhatsApp-style chat interface.

The goal is to make important public information accessible, easy to understand, and available to everyone, including users who may not be comfortable with complex government portals.

Users interact with the system just like a messaging app, asking questions and receiving guidance step-by-step.

Features
WhatsApp-Style Chat Interface

A familiar chat layout that makes the platform easy to use for anyone.

Conversational Assistance

Users can ask about:

Legal rights

Farmer schemes

Government benefits

The assistant responds with helpful guidance in a conversational format.

Quick Reply Buttons

Users can instantly explore common topics such as:

Know my rights

Farmer schemes

Government benefits

Mobile-First Design

The interface is optimized for mobile users and works well on smaller screens.

Simulated AI Responses

Predefined responses simulate a conversational assistant without requiring external AI APIs.

Language Selector (UI)

A dropdown allows users to choose languages for future multilingual support.

Chat Features

Chat bubbles similar to WhatsApp

User and assistant messages

Message timestamps

Scrollable chat history

Typing delay to simulate real conversation
